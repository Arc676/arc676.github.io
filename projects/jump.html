Jump is based on the XKCD comic of the same name. It was created for the XKCD Game Jam. The objective is to collect all the yellow spheres. It's a simple game, but the primary mechanic is that when you jump, gravity only slows you down until your vertical velocity is 0. Press SPACE for longer to jump higher.

Jump was created using the Unity game engine. Platform availability:

  • Mac
  • Windows
  • Linux
