Crystal Ball randomly answers your yes/no questions. If you're ever unsure about something or want to pick from several options, Crystal Ball can help. DISCLAIMER: Please don't use Crystal Ball for any serious decision making. Answers are chosen pseudorandomly and should definitely not be used for important choices. Please consult a human being, preferably one with expertise in the relevant field, if you need to make such a decision and are unsure.

Platform availability:

  • iOS (but not on the App Store; same translations as the Mac version)
  • Mac (available in English, Italian, Simplified Chinese, German, and French)
  • Linux, Ubuntu Touch (see OpenStore page for available translations)
